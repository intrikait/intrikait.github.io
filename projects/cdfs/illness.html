Physical Disability

A physical impairment which has a substantial and long term effect on one's ability to carry out day-to-day activities.

Chronic Disease

A disease or condition lasting more than 3 months.

Mental Illness

A disorder that physically affects the mind and influences mood, thinking, and behavior.
